ABOUT THE FEST

Teghveer is a celebration of courage, leadership, and youthful spirit, inspired by the timeless bravery of Sri Guru Tegh Bahadur Ji. Rooted in the core values of discipline, resilience, and service, Teghveer reflects the ethos of NCC and the proud identity of GTBIT. This fest brings together energy, creativity, and camaraderie, providing a platform for students to showcase their talents, challenge their limits, and grow as responsible individuals.
